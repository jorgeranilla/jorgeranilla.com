About Me

Hello, I’m Jorge.

I was born and raised in Peru and moved to the United States at a young age in search of new opportunities and growth. Over time, this country became home, and I am proud to be American by choice.

Professionally, I work in the banking and operations field, where I value structure, accountability, and continuous improvement. I enjoy solving problems, improving processes, and taking pride in doing things the right way. Learning has always been important to me, whether through formal education, hands-on experience, or simply figuring things out along the way.

Outside of work, family comes first. I am a husband and a dad, and I deeply value the time spent with the people I love. This website reflects that. It is a place to preserve memories, share updates, and document our journey together as a growing family.

I also enjoy traveling and exploring new cultures. These experiences have shaped how I see the world and helped me appreciate different perspectives.

This site is meant to be simple and personal. It serves as a digital home for both my professional path and my family’s story.
