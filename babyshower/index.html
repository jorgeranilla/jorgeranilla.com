What Happened?

An unexpected turn of events.

Baby Shower Scheduled for February 22nd - Cancelled

We need to share some unexpected news with our family. Shane was admitted to WakeMed Women's Pavilion in Cary, NC this week at 33 weeks pregnant with active preterm labor. She's receiving exceptional care from an amazing team of medical professionals who have shown us incredible compassion and love. Both she and baby Alyssa are stable and being closely monitored.

Shane is on magnesium sulfate to help stop contractions, receiving IV fluids, and has completed corticosteroid injections to help Alyssa's lungs develop. The medical team is working to delay delivery for at least 24-48 hours to give the steroids time to work and give Alyssa more time to grow.

Because Shane will remain hospitalized until delivery, we've made the difficult decision to cancel the baby shower that was planned for February 22nd. My place is with Shane right now, and we wanted to let everyone know as soon as possible out of respect for your time.

To those who RSVP'd and those who have already sent gifts - thank you from the bottom of our hearts. Your love and support mean everything to us, especially now.

We're taking things day by day and trusting in God's plan. We'll keep you posted on Alyssa's arrival.

Thank you for your understanding and continued prayers.
Shane & Jorge
