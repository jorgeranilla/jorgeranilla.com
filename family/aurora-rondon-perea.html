Aurora Rondon Perea

Deceased | Great-Grandmother

Aurora Rondon Perea was a woman of strength and warmth whose love for her family defined everything she was. From Arequipa, she built a home and a life alongside Sergio Ranilla Ranilla, together becoming the foundation of the Ranilla family.

She raised her children with devotion and passed on the values of hard work, loyalty, and family unity that continue to shape us today. Her spirit lives on in all who carry her memory.

Though little was written about her life, her love was felt across generations. We honor her as the great-grandmother whose quiet strength made everything that came after possible.
