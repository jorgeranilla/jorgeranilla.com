"Tio Llocsi"

Uncle

A cherished uncle whose warmth and kindness touched everyone he met. Tio Llocsi brought joy and laughter to our family gatherings. His memory remains a blessing to all who knew him.

Though he is no longer with us, his love, humor, and generous spirit continue to guide our family. We honor his memory and celebrate the beautiful life he lived.

His presence endures in our hearts, in the stories we share, and in the values he instilled in us. He will never be forgotten.
