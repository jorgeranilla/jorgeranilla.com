Hector Briceno

Forever loved, forever remembered, forever part of our family.

My cousin Hector's life was much shorter than any of us would have wished, but the love that surrounded him and the memories he left behind continue to live on within our family.

From the very beginning, Hector faced significant health challenges that required extraordinary care and attention. Throughout his life, he was surrounded by the unwavering love and devotion of his parents, Hector and Janet. Their commitment to him was constant, and they dedicated themselves wholeheartedly to ensuring that he was cared for, protected, and loved every single day.

One of the things I remember most when thinking about Hector is not the challenges he faced, but the love that surrounded him. His mother, Janet, became an extraordinary example of a parent's devotion. She dedicated herself completely to caring for her son, showing a level of patience, strength, and unconditional love that inspired everyone around her. His father, Hector, whose name he proudly carried, was also a constant source of support, love, and dedication throughout his life.

Although his time with us was brief, Hector remains deeply loved and remembered by his parents, relatives, cousins, aunts, and uncles. He was, and continues to be, an important part of our family's story. His memory lives on through the people who knew him and through the love that continues to surround his name.

When our family gathers and remembers those who are no longer with us, Hector is always among them. He is remembered with affection, tenderness, and a smile. His life reminds us of the importance of family, compassion, and cherishing every moment we have with the people we love.

To me, Hector will always be one of the angels of our family. Though he is no longer physically with us, his memory remains in our hearts, and he continues to be loved by all those whose lives he touched. His life may have been short, but the love he inspired has lasted far beyond his years and continues to unite the family that will always remember him.
