In Loving Memory

Though they are no longer with us, their love, wisdom, and spirit continue to guide our family. We honor their memory and celebrate the beautiful lives they lived.

Their presence endures in our hearts, in the stories we share, and in the values they instilled in us. They will never be forgotten.

"Those we love don't go away, they walk beside us every day. Unseen, unheard, but always near, still loved, still missed, and very dear."

Eugenio Astocondor Salazar

"Papaqueno"
1918 - 2000
Grandfather
A pillar of strength and wisdom in our family. His gentle spirit and unwavering love shaped generations. Papaqueno's legacy lives on in the values he taught us and the memories we cherish.

A cherished uncle whose warmth and kindness touched everyone he met. Tio Llocsi brought joy and laughter to our family gatherings. His memory remains a blessing to all who knew him.

Aunt
A loving aunt whose caring nature and generous heart made her beloved by all. Tia Titi's presence brought comfort and joy to our family. She will forever be remembered with love and gratitude.

"Mama Carlota"
1911 - 2008
Great-Grandmother
A matriarch whose strength and grace guided our family through generations. Mama Carlota's wisdom and unconditional love created a foundation that continues to support us. Her spirit lives on in all of us.

Maria Jesus Cateriano Dongo

Sergio Raul Ranilla Rondon
