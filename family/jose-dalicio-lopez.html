Jose Dalicio Lopez Lopez

"Papa Dalicio"

1908 - 1990 | Great-Grandfather

A man of quiet dignity and enduring strength, Jose Dalicio Lopez Lopez was the patriarch of a family rooted in the mountains of Rodriguez de Mendoza, Amazonas. His life was one of hard work, sacrifice, and deep love for his family.

Though he is no longer with us, his legacy lives on through the generations he helped shape. His determination and character set the foundation for the family that followed. We honor the memory of this man whose journey made ours possible.

His presence endures in our hearts, in the stories we share, and in the values he instilled in those he loved. He will never be forgotten.
