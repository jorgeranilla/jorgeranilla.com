Maria Jesus Cateriano Dongo

1936 - 2023 | Grandmother

A devoted grandmother whose love knew no bounds. Her kindness, faith, and dedication to family were an inspiration to all. She leaves behind a legacy of love that will never fade.

Though she is no longer with us, her love, wisdom, and spirit continue to guide our family. We honor her memory and celebrate the beautiful life she lived.

Her presence endures in our hearts, in the stories we share, and in the values she instilled in us. She will never be forgotten.
