Sergio Ranilla Ranilla

1901 - Deceased | Great-Grandfather

Sergio Ranilla Ranilla was born in 1901 in the port region of Camaná, Arequipa, on the southern coast of Peru. A man shaped by the sea and the sun, he carried the resilience and hardworking spirit that defined the coastal communities of his time.

His life was one of quiet dedication to his work, to his family, and to the values he passed down through the generations. He is the great-grandfather from whom the Ranilla name in our family descends.

Though history recorded little about him, his legacy is carried forward in every generation that followed. We honor the memory of the man whose roots became ours.
