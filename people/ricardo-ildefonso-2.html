Ricardo Ildefonso

What I appreciate most about Ricardo is not only the history we share, but also the way I have been able to rely on his mind and judgment. Ricardo has always been very strong with numbers and financial thinking. When I was preparing to buy my first home, I consulted him often to help me think through the terms, the options, and the decisions in front of me. His feedback was valuable because he has a natural ability to look at numbers carefully and see things from a practical, informed perspective.

Over the years, I have continued to value his opinion, especially when it comes to financial decisions. He is someone whose advice I take seriously because he does not just give an answer. He helps you think through the details. That kind of perspective is rare, and it is one of the many reasons I respect him.

Ricardo is a close friend and a trusted voice in my life. We have shared years of travel, laughter, important decisions, and memories that go back to some of the earliest chapters of my life in the United States. I am grateful for the bond we have built over so many years and for the many moments that continue to connect our stories.
