Quick Answers

How do I choose a size?

Product pages list available sizes. If you are between sizes, email before ordering and I can help you pick the better fit.

How does shipping work?

Standard shipping is 5-7 business days. Orders over $75 ship free.

Can I return something?

Unworn items can be returned within 30 days. Send an email with your order details and I will help from there.

How should I care for the pieces?

Wash apparel cold, avoid bleach, and hang dry or tumble dry low. For accessories, spot clean gently.

Do you take custom ideas?

Yes. If a piece, phrase, or small run would make sense for Bloomsai Co., send the idea over.

Small-batch pieces inspired by growth, roots, and the connections we build throughout our lives.
